Hemant Fasteners was set up in 2012 under the able leadership of its founding chairman, Mr. Jitender Singh. From nothing to many things, our company has shown tremendous growth in past years and secured a name in the market. Our company started with manufacturing of only one component, but due to our passion, hard work and customers trust we are now dealing with number of components and the number is still increasing. With a vision to provide high quality nuts, screws and bolts to industries world-wide, we embarked upon a journey that has led us to become one of the suppliers of fastener solutions producing the widest range of precision cold forged low and high tensile industrial fasteners
